CHAPTER 3 - AN ACT TO DEFINE THE NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF BAUSTRALIA

PREFACE

Whereas the Parliament of Baustralia has repealed all the acts of Parliament for restructuring, we, the Parliament are issuing this act, to declare all national symbols.
This Act shall be known as National Symbols Act, 2018.
Provisions of this Act referring to His Majesty the King extend also to the Heirs and Successors of His Majesty, Kings and Queens of the Kingdom of Baustralia.

FLAG

-HORIZONTAL HOISTING

The flag of Baustralia shall be blazoned as follows: Tierced in pale, azure argent azure, in a ratio of 1:2:1. In pale a Baustralian fish naiant of the first.
Azure shall be defined as follows:

ROYAL ARMS

The royal arms of Baustralia shall be defined as follows: 
    For escutcheon, Quarterly, in the first and fourth sable a Baustralian fish naiant azure, in the second gules a riel or, in the third of the second the letter W inscribed of the fourth.
    For supporters, lions rampant guardant royally crowned all proper.
    For compartment, upon a mound vert a stream azure therein a Baustralian fish naiant azure.
    Mantling azure and ermine.
    For a crest, upon the royal helm the royal crown proper, thereon a lion statant guardant or royally crowned proper.

HONOURS MUSIC

-ROYAL ANTHEM

The royal anthem shall be God Save the King and its lyrics and melody is below.

God save our gracious King!
Long live our noble King!
God save the King!
Send him victorious,
Happy and glorious,
Long to reign over us:
God save the King!
