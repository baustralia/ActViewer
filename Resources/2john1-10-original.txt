CHAPTER 10 - AN ACT TO MAKE PROVISION FOR AND IN CONNECTION WITH THE ENDING OF BAUSTRALIAN SOVEREIGNTY AND JURISDICTION OVER THE FOX ISLANDS

TERMS

1.   Be it enacted by the King’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—

ENDING OF SOVEREIGNITY

2.   As from 1 May 2019, His Majesty shall no longer have sovereignty or jurisdiction over any part of the Fox Islands.

SHORT TITLE AND PROVISIONS

3.   This Act may be cited as the Fox Islands Act.
4.   The Schedule to this Act shall have effect with respect to the matters there mentioned, being matters consequential on or connected with the provisions of section 1 above.

SCHEDULE

5.   In this Schedule "the relevant date" means 1 May 2019 and "enactment" means any provision of an Act, other than this Act, passed before the relevant date and any provision of an instrument made before that date under any such Act.
