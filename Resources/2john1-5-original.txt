CHAPTER 5 - AN ACT TO RENAME HIS ROYAL ARMY TO BAUSTRALIAN ARMY

TERMS

Whereas His Majesty wishes for the Armed Forces to be more universal, the Army shall be renamed Baustralian Army.
This Act shall be known as Army Act, 2018.
Provisions of this Act referring to His Majesty the King extend also to the Heirs and Successors of His Majesty, Kings and Queens of the Kingdom of Baustralia.
