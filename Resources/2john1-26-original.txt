CHAPTER 26 - AN ACT TO DISSOLVE HIS ROYAL AIR FORCE AND REPLACE IT WITH HIS ROYAL FLYING CORPS AND THE NAVAL AIR ARM 

PREFACE

1.	 Whereas the Air Force have dropped in NCO strength to four (4), His Majesty’s House of Lords Temporal and Spiritual wishes for the air force to muster in the ranks with the navy.
2.   This Act shall be known as the Air Force Dissolution Act, 2019.

TERMS

3.	 His Royal Flying Corps (HRFC) shall be formed under command of the Army
4.	 His Royal Air Force shall be dissolved, and their strength muster with either the Naval Air Arm or HRFC.
5.	 The army cap badge will remain the same design as His Royal Air Force.
