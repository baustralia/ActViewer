CHAPTER 13 - AN ACT TO AMEND THE ACT OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING THE UNION WITH QUEBEC DUE TO A CHANGE IN NAME AND GOVERNMENT

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty and by the consent of the Commons as follows:-
1.   Whereas the Second Kingdom of Quebec and Versaillies has changed their name to the United People's Federation of Quebec, the Union with Quebec Act, 3 John 1 c. 9 must be amended for the Act to work in the future.
2.   This Act shall be known as the Union with Quebec (Modifications) Act (Baustralia), 2019.

AMENDMENTS

3.   After paragraph three, the following shall be inserted: While this Act refers to the Second Kingdom of Quebec and Versailles, this shall include any changes to name, form of government, or change of head of state.
