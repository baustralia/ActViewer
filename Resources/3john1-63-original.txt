CHAPTER 63 - AN ACT TO BRING INTO FORCE THE TREATY OF NUEVO HOLDERTONA

WHEREAS, the Presidency of the Free Socialist State of Paloma wishes to recognize the Kingdom and Empire of Baustralia, be it enacted the following:-
This Act shall be known as the Treaty of Nuevo Holdertona Act, 2020.

SCHEDULE

TREATY OF NUEVO HOLDERTONA FOR MUTUAL RECOGNITION BETWEEN BAUSTRALIA AND PALOMA

1.   This treaty serves to provide mutual recognition between the Empire of Baustralia and the Free Socialist State of Paloma (“the nations concerned”). 
2.   The nations concerned will recognize each other in accordance with international law.
3.   The nations concerned agree to defend each other if need be.
