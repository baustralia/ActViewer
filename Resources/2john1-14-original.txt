CHAPTER 14 - AN ACT TO FORMALLY RECOGNIZE THE INDEPENDENCE OF RMHOANIA

PREFACE

Be it known that His Most Excellent and Gracious Majesty has, with the advice of His ministers, accepted the independence of London and sovereignty of Rmhoania.

TERMS

1.   This Act shall be known as the Rmhoania Act 2019.
2.   The province of London shall be independent.
3.   The sovereignty of Rmhoania shall not be affected by this Bill.
4.   Citizens of Rmhoania may also serve as Regular and Reserve members of His Majesty’s Baustralian Armed Forces.
5.   Baustralia has no jurisdiction over Rmhoania, her lands, nor her people.
6.   Rmhoania has two monarchs, co-ruling to create a Diarchy.
