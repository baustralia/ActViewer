CHAPTER 17 - AN ACT TO AMEND THE THIRD CHAPTER OF THE STATUTE OF PARLIAMENT THAT SAT DURING THE SIXTH YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, and with the Consent of the Current Commons assembled, the following:-
1.   This Act shall be referred to as the Secretaries of State (No. 2) Act, 2022, C661.
2.   The first Section of the Third Chapter of the Statute of Parliament that sat during the Sixth Year of the Reign of King John shall be amended to read: 
       This Act shall be known as the Secretaries of State (No. 1) Act, 2022, Bill C611.
