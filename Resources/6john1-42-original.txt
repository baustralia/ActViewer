CHAPTER 42 - AN ACT TO AMEND THE THIRTY-FIFTH CHAPTER OF THE STATUTE OF PARLIAMENT THAT SAT DURING THE SIXTH YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN, TO PROVIDE FOR THE FLAG LIEUTENANTS AND AIDES-DE-CAMP FOR COMMODORES AND BRIGADIERS

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, the following:-
1.   This Act will be known as the Aides-de-Camp (Commodores and Brigadiers) Act, T6201, 2023.
2.   The following shall be inserted to the Thirty-Fifth Chapter of the Statute of Parliament that sat during the Sixth Year of the Reign of King John:-
 13.  Brigadiers may appoint a major to be an aide-de-camp.
 14.  Commodores may appoint a lieutenant commander to be a flag lieutenant.
