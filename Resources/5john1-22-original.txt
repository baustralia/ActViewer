CHAPTER 22 - AN ACT TO CONFIRM THE STATUS OF THE FOURTY SIXTH CHAPTER OF THE STATUTE OF THE PARLIAMENT THAT SAT DURING THE THIRD YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN I, THE PUBLIC HEALTH EMERGENCY MEASURES (QUARANTINE) ACT

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, and with the Consent of the Current Commons assembled, the following:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Public Health Emergency Measures (Confirmation) Act, 2022, C-51.
2.   3 John 1 c, 46, the Public Health Emergency Measures (Quarantine) Act, shall be considered spent in accordance with section two of the aforementioned Act of Parliament.
