PREFACE

1.   This act shall be added to the Baustralian Criminal Code.
2.   This Act may be cited as the Counterfeit Act, 2019.

TERMS

3.   Counterfeit currency is illegal in Baustralia and is punishable by fine up to 200% of the value of currency made.
4.   One found guilty of counterfeiting currency must personally pay the crown one Boerc.
