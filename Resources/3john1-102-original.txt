CHAPTER 102 - AN ACT REGARDING THE CONSTITUENCIES OF SAYVILLE, TO GIVE UP BAUSTRALIAN SOVEREIGNITY THEREIN, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Whereas, by request of the members of the Constituency and Province of Sayville, be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, the following:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Constituencies (No. 3) Act, 2020.
2.   The Province of Sayville will be dissolved.
3.   The single person making up the population of Sayville will be moved to the Constituency of Landspotter.
4.   This Act shall come into force on 20 June at 12:00 noon, Eastern Daylight Time (UTC-4:00).
