CHAPTER 95 - AN ACT REGARDING THE PROVINCES OF HOLDERTON, OF LONDON, OF SMITHFIELD, OF LAYUS ISLAND, OF NOVA AND OF DYNEXISTAN, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Whereas the Parliament is becoming vacant, BE IT ENACTED by His Most Gracious Majesty the following:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Provinces Act, 2020.
2.   These provinces will be dissolved, and the land given back to the macronation thereof.
 a)   Layus Island,
 b)   Nova, and
 c)   Dynexistan.
3.   Smithfield will merge with Holderton.
4.   London will merge with Holderton.
