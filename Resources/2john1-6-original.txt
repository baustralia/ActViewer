CHAPTER 6 - AN ACT TO MAKE HIS ROYAL AIR FORCE SUBORDINATE TO THE BAUSTRALIAN ARMY

TERMS

Whereas His Majesty wishes for the Baustralian Army to be higher in strength, His Royal Air Force shall become a command of the Baustralian Army, known as the Air Command.
This Act shall be known as Air Force Act, 2018.
Provisions of this Act referring to His Majesty the King extend also to the Heirs and Successors of His Majesty, Kings and Queens of the Kingdom of Baustralia.
