CHAPTER 82 - AN ACT TO TRANSFER THE POSITION OF SECRETARY OF STATE FOR THE SECRET SERVICE TO THE BAUSTRALIAN ARMED FORCES AS COLONEL COMMANDANT, KINGDOM’S SECRET SERVICE BRIGADE, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

BE IT ENACTED by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty as follows:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Secretary of State for the Secret Service Act, 2020.
2.   As the Kingdom’s Secret Service Brigade was transferred to the Baustralian Armed Forces, this Act will transfer the office to the Baustralian Armed Forces.
3.   The Secretary of State will become the colonel commandant of the Kingdom’s Secret Service Brigade.
