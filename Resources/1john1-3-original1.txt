CHAPTER 1 - AN ACT TO REPEAL ALL BILLS

PREFACE

Whereas His Most Excellent Majesty wishes to restructure all laws, with this act, all previous bills shall all be repealed.
This Act shall be known as Repeal Act, 2018.
Provisions of this Act referring to His Majesty the King extend also to the Heirs and Successors of His Majesty, Kings and Queens of the Kingdom of Baustralia.

TERMS

These acts shall be repealed and shall have no affect over the people of Baustralia.
