CHAPTER 18 - AN ACT REGARDING THE CITATIONS OF THE THE THIRD CHAPTER OF THE STATUTE OF THE PARLIAMENTARY SESSION THAT SAT IN THE FIRST YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN I, COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE MODIFICATION ACT TO THE SUCCESSION LAW, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious Majesty, and with the Consent of the Current Commons assembled, after the destruction of the original Act, the following:
1.   This Act shall be known as the Citation (Succession Law) Act, c. 18.
2.   1 John 1 c. 3 shall be amended, such that the short title reads: Succession Law (Modifications) Act.
