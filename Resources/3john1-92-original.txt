CHAPTER 92 - AN ACT TO CONDEMN THE EMPIRE OF KAPREBURG, TO REMOVE RECOGNITION THEREOF, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Excellent Majesty and by the Consent of the Commons the following:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Kapreburg Act, 2020.
2.   Whereas in the Great Lakes Council Court of Justice, the Tsar of Kapreburg had been most unprofessional and was deemed childish, and that the ruling of the Council was to have all members remove recognition status, this Act shall be written proof thereof.
