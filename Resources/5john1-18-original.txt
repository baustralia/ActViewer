CHAPTER 18 - AN ACT REGARDING THE BILL CITATIONS USED IN THE PARLIAMENT OF BAUSTRALIA, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious Majesty as follows:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Citation (Bills) Act, 2022, T-50.
2.   Bill citations in the Parliament shall take the form of:
 a)   “Bill”
 b)   Legislature originally introduced
  i)   “C-”, Commons
  ii)  “L-”, Lords
  iii) “T-”, Throne
  iv)  “P-”, Knights, Flag and General officers, and civil servants.
 c)   Regnal year
 d)   Number of bill
3.   All Acts of Parliament will be marked with the bill citation in descending order by reading, i.e., 3rd, 2nd, 1st reading, e.g. C-548, C-524, C-512.
