CHAPTER 44 - AN ACT RESPECTING SHORT TITLES OF TWO ACTS PASSED IN THE 2ND YEAR OF HIS MAJESTY’S REIGN RESPECTING MODIFICATIONS TO THE SUCCESSION LAW

Be it enacted by the King’s most Excellent Majesty, in this present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:— 
1.   This Act may be cited as the Short Titles (No. 2) Act, 2020.
2.   2 John 1 c. 15, paragraph 1, shall be amended to read: “This Act shall be known as the Air Force (No. 2) Act 2019.”
3.   All Acts which have the same short title as another in the same regnal year will be numbered in the same format as this Act, the Air Force (No. 2) Act, and the Succession Law (Modifications, No. 2) Act.
