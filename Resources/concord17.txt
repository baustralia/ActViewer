Signed at Concord, HM District, Baustralia, December 27 2017

Text for the treaty is lost.

It provided for the end of battle between the Kingdom of Baustralia and the Republic of Wangatangia, for the republic's recognition of their unprovoked attack, and for the status of Wangtangia to become a Dominion of Baustralia.

It was signed by King John and Jacob Mayjames, now the Viscount Mayjames.
