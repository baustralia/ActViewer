CHAPTER 85 - AN ACT REGARDING THE REPEAL OF THE FORTY-EIGHT CHAPTER OF THE SESSION OF PARLIAMENT THAT SAT DURING THE THIRD YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN THE FIRST AND THE FIFTY-EIGHTH CHAPTER OF THE SESSION OF PARLIAMENT THAT SAT DURING THE THIRD YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN THE FIRST, TO APPLY RESTRICTIONS TO AIDAN JOHN MCGRATH, AND PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

BE IT ENACTED by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, and the current Parliament assembled as follows:-

TERMS

1.   This Act shall be known as the Persona Grata (McGrath) Act, 2020.
2.   Persona Non Grata (McGrath) Act, 3 John 1 c. 48, shall be repealed.
3.   Paloma (Citizenship) Act, 3 John 1 c. 54, shall be repealed.
4.   Aidan McGrath may be allowed citizenship in Baustralia.
5.   Aidan McGrath will be restricted from acting in government.
6.   Aidan McGrath will not have the right to vote in general elections in the Kingdom of Baustralia.
