CHAPTER 7 - AN ACT REGARDING THE NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF WANGATANGIA

PREFACE

1.   This Act, less section 5 shall extend to Wangatangia only.
2.   Section 5 shall extend to the whole empire.
3.   This Act shall be known as the National Symbols Act (Wangatangia), 2019.

TERMS

4.   The flag of Wangatangia shall be a Baustralian red ensign, that is a red flag with the Baustralian flag in the canton, with the escutcheon in the fly.
5.   The flag shall be treated with the same respect as the Baustralian flag.
6.   The coat of arms of Wangatangia shall be described as follows:-
 a)   Escutcheon: Quarterly,
  I.   Azure a ‘W’ serifed or
  II.  Gules a riel or
  III. Sable a Baustralian fish naiant azure 
  IV.  As the first
 b)   Supporters: Two dogs
 c)   Compartment: In front of a Canadian and Baustralian flag, a mount vert.
 d)   Crest: Upon a royal helm, a lion royally crowned in his dexter forepaw the flag of Wangatangia standing upon a torse argent, blue, celeste, and tenné
 e)   Mantling: Gules and Azure
 f)   Motto: Snitches are bitches
