CHAPTER 3 - AN ACT REGARDING THE PUNISHMENT OF INFORMANTS WITHIN WANGATANGIA

PREFACE

1.   This Act shall be known as the Informants Act (Wangatangia), 2019.
2.   This Act shall extend only to Wangatangia.

TERMS

3.   An informant may also be known by the colloquial term “snitch.”
4.   Informants in Wangatangia shall be punished by a 1,000 Wangatang Boerc fine.
5.   This alludes to the motto “snitches are bitches.”
