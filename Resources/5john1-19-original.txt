CHAPTER 19 - AN ACT REGARDING ACTIVITIES DEEMED ACCEPTABLE WITHIN THE PARLIAMENT, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty, and with the consent of the Current Commons assembled:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Political Activities Act, 2022, C-50.
2.   No member shall buy, or coerce someone through employment, promotion, or other bribes, to vote for a candidate or party.
3.   No member shall be a member of any political organization which advocates the overthrow of our constitutional form of government.
4.   A member who is in violation of this Act will be dismissed from the Parliament.
5.   No criminal charges will be laid against a member who is in violation of this Act.
6.   Any political party which aligns with the far-right or far-left, i.e. authoritarian, fascist, anarchist, or communist parties, are considered an organization in violation of section 3 of this Act.
