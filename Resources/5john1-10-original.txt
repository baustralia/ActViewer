CHAPTER 10 - AN ACT REGARDING THE CURRENT COUNTY AND FORMER PROTECTORATE OF ÎLE AUX COULEUVRES AND TO GRANT SUCH ISLAND AND TERRITORY OF THE KING IN RIGHT OF BAUSTRALIA TO THE CROWN AND EMPIRE OF KAPREBURG, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

Be it enacted by His Most Gracious Majesty as follows:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Île aux Couleuvres Act, 2022.
2.   The County which comprises the former Protectorate of the Île aux Couleuvres in the Kingdom of Baustralia shall be granted to the Crown and Empire of Kapreburg.
