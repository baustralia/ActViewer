CHAPTER 11 - AN ACT TO REPEAL THE MODIFICATIONS TO THE SUCCESSION LAW OF BAUSTRALIA AND REPLACE IT WITH A LESS RESTRICTIVE METHOD

PREFACE

1.   Whereas in the past, His Most Excellent and Gracious Majesty had put forth a bill barring Their Royal Highnesses from succession, he feels that it may be beneficial to revert to a standard law.
2.   This Act shall be known as the “Modification Act to the Succession Law of Baustralia, 2019”

TERMS

3.   Their Royal Highnesses may reign as King or Queen of the Baustralians, the Dominions, and Emperor/Empress of Ostreum.
4.   Her Royal Highness and her decendants shall be allowed to reign once again in Edstmae, however, she shall remain abdicated.
5.   2 John 1 c. 4 shall be revoked.
6.   HM-PPU-001 shall remain revoked.
7.   The succession law shall remain as male-preference primogeniture.
8.   The heir presumptive shall be Her Royal Highness, and shall reign as Her Majesty Katelynn, by the Grace of God of Baustralia, the Dominions, Queen, Empress of Ostreum, or in Latin as Eius Maiestas Katelynn Dei Gratia Baustralia, terrarum quae in ditione sunt Baustralia Regina, Ostrei Imperatrix.

SCHEDULE

9.   Their Royal Highnesses in this bill shall refer to His Royal Highness, John, the Prince of Kingston, Her Royal Highness, Susan, the Princess of Kingston, Her Royal Highness, Susan, the Princess of Wabasso.
10.  Her Royal Highness in this bill shall refer to Her Royal Highness, Katelynn, the Princess of Wabasso.
