CHAPTER 79 - AN ACT REGARDING PROSTITUTION IN THE EMPIRE OF OSTREUM, TO REPEAL THE SEVENTEENTH CHAPTER OF THE PARLIAMENTARY SESSION THAT SAT DURING THE THIRD YEAR OF THE REIGN OF KING JOHN I, AND FOR PURPOSES CONNECTED THEREWITH

BE IT ENACTED by His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty and the current Commons assembled, the following:-
1.   This Act shall be known as the Prostitution Act (Ostreum), 2020.
2.   The Seventeenth Chapter of the Parliamentary Session that sat during the Third Year of the Reign of King John I, an Act to de-extend the Prostitution Act in Ostreum, the Prostitution (Legalization) Act (Ostreum), 2019, shall hereby be repealed with immediate effect.
3.   Prostitution will be illegal in Ostreum under the provisions of the Prostitution Act and the Prostitution (Fines) Act of the Third Year of the Reign of King John I.
