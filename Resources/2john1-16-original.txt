CHAPTER 16 - AN ACT TO ALLOW THE MONARCH TO PRACTICE WHATEVER RELIGION HE MAY WISH

PREFACE

Be it known that His Most Gracious and Excellent Majesty has set out in an Order-in-Council that all heirs and successors must be in communion with the Christian God.

TERMS

1.   This Act shall be known as the Religion Act 2019.
2.   The succession law shall remain the same.
3.   Heirs may become monarch regardless of what religion he may practice.
4.   Heirs may also become monarch if they are atheist.
5.   The spouses or consorts of the monarch and heirs may also practice whatever religion they may wish.
6.   Spouses and consorts of the monarch and heirs may also be atheist.
